Welcome to International Day by Noor Language School

Discover the uniqueness of Yemen, Italy, Mexico, and Japan.

Yemen

Yemen is known for its ancient culture, stunning architecture, and breathtaking landscapes. Visit the old city of Sana’a, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, or explore the biodiversity of Socotra Island.

Italy

Italy is the birthplace of the Renaissance and home to magnificent landmarks like the Colosseum, Venice canals, and Vatican City. Enjoy the delicious cuisine, from pasta to pizza.

Mexico

Mexico offers vibrant traditions, stunning beaches, and rich history. Visit ancient Mayan ruins or enjoy colorful festivals like Día de los Muertos.

Japan

Japan blends ancient traditions with futuristic innovation. From cherry blossoms to the bustling streets of Tokyo, there’s something for everyone.
